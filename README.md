Results Summary

Top Strategy: Absolute Mean Reversion with 1% Stop-Loss.

Performance: Cumulative PnL of 23.20.

Key Insight: Tight risk management (1% stop-loss) outperformed wider stops (5% or 10%) in modern S&P 500 conditions, proving that minimizing drawdown is key to statistical arbitrage.
